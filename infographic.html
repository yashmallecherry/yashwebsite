The infographic presents key statistics on mental health during the 2020 pandemic, highlighting the prevalence of issues and their impact on individuals' well-being. In 2020, 21% of adults experienced mental health conditions, with 5.6% facing serious issues. Treatment-seeking increased to 21.6% in 2021, while 16.5% used prescription medication. Mental health medication prices rose by 12%, potentially posing economic challenges for treatment access. The infographic also offers pro tips for mental health awareness, including staying active, managing stress, staying hydrated, working out consistently, practicing outdoor yoga and meditation, and connecting with nature.
