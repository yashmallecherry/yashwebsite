05Museum Rebranding
In the museum rebranding project, I transformed an unknown museum located in Satara into a digital powerhouse. By creating a user-friendly website, I effectively showcased the museum's extensive collection of articles and artwork to a wider audience. To enhance visitor experience and create lasting memories, I introduced a unique feature: customized digital receipts that visitors could keep as mementos of their visit. Additionally, I designed intuitive gallery icons for each exhibition, ensuring convenient navigation and understanding of the museum's diverse offerings. Through these strategic initiatives, the museum underwent a successful rebranding, elevating its profile and accessibility in the cultural landscape.
