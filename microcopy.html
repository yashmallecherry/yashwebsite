07Microcopy Canvas
The Microcopy Canvas focuses on enhancing the IPO selection process for SBI and Mobile App users, catering primarily to professionals,students, and parents aged 20-60. By simplifying language, utilizing arrow symbols for navigation, and adjusting font sizes and alignment, the goal is to alleviate user concerns related to confusion and frustration, particularly among older users. Emphasizing a professional yet simple tone and aligning with common design patterns, the microcopy aims to facilitate a seamless and intuitive experience. Success will be measured by reduced confusion, increased completion rates, and positive feedback on improved readability and alignment.
